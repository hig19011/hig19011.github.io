By Necia P. Seamons

The Idaho Transportation Department (ITD) and the National Weather Service (NWS) are advising the traveling public to use caution while in transit due to an extended severe winter weather advisory beginning this evening Wed., Feb. 5.

Drivers can expect high winds, blowing and drifting snow, low visibilty and heavy snow in higher elevations.
