Avalanches Come Early This Year

By Iwana J. Gitout

Five people have been killed in three avalanches in Idaho and Eastern Oregon within the last week. In at least one case, the avalanche was proven to be human-triggered.

Scott Savage, director of the Sawtooth Avalanche Center, said there is no average time frame that is considered "avalanche season" so having multiple avalanches in January is not unusual.

Savage said the center sees hundreds of avalanches each season that are big enough to bury a person and typically, at least 10 of those are human-triggered.
